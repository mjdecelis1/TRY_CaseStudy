THE JOURNEY BEHIND.

Artvelfun was born from a shared passion for showcasing the beauty and diversity of the Philippines. Created by five determined college students—united not only by their love for travel but also by the goal of successfully finishing this semester—Artvelfun is more than just a project; it's a celebration of the country's wonders.

Our blog highlights the best of the Philippines through vivid stories, stunning photography, and practical travel tips. From the bustling cities of Luzon to the serene shores of Visayas, and the untamed beauty of Mindanao, we’ve sorted destinations into three main geographical divisions to help you explore with ease.

But Artvelfun isn’t just about places—it’s about experiences. We feature local cuisine, vibrant cultural festivities, and authentic stories from fellow travelers in our community section. Here, readers can discover genuine insights and share their own adventures.

Whether you’re an adventurer seeking your next thrill, a foodie exploring local flavors, or someone planning your dream trip to the Philippines, Artvelfun offers inspiration and practical guidance to make every journey unforgettable.

Join us as we explore, learn, and share the beauty of the Philippines—while crossing our fingers to ace this semester!
